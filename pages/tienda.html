Sarastrada

Precio del artículo ❤ 10

The design of this family is inspired by the types of wood used in nineteenth-century advertising posters with unconventional shapes, developing a calligraphic model to create shapes and understand construction, endowing each phrase with expressiveness. The inverted contrast and strong stems of this family are suitable for different design projects, branding, packaging, poster, editorial de- sign, etc. The family is ideal for designers around the world as it has 640 glyphs in its 5 weight (Black, Bold, Medium, Regular and Thin) that cover more than 200 languages.
